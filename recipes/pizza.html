Pizza

Description

In the picture above There is a Pizza. It has dough on the bottom made of flour, sauce on top of the dough from tomatoe or can buy it called tomatoe sauce, Cheese on top of the tomatoe sauce, and Pepperoni on top of the cheese and everything else.

Ingridients for making the Pizza

  • Flour For Dough
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni (Optional)
  • Pizza Cutter Or Knife

Recipe For Creating Pizza

  1. Make The Dough
  2. Proof the Dough
  3. Prepare the Sauce and toppings
  4. Shape the dough
  5. Bake the Pizza
  6. Cool the Pizza
  7. Cut the Pizza Evenly
